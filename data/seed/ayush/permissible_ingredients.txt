AYUSH Permissible Ingredients Schedule

Section 2.1 — Schedule H: Dosage Caps for Common Herbal Ingredients (per serving, adults)
Ashwagandha root extract: 600mg
Tulsi leaf extract: 500mg
Turmeric (curcumin standardized): 500mg
Brahmi extract: 300mg
Shatavari extract: 450mg

Section 2.2 — Reduced Caps for Children's Products
When a product's stated target demographic includes "children", the above caps are reduced by 60% and a healthcare-consultation caution statement is mandatory on the package.

Section 2.3 — Combination Products
When two or more herbal ingredients are combined in a single product, the cumulative dosage must not exceed 80% of the sum of individual caps, to account for potential interaction effects.

Section 2.4 — Elderly Demographic
Products targeting the elderly demographic do not require reduced caps but must include an interaction warning if the ingredient list includes Ashwagandha (potential interaction with thyroid and blood pressure medication) or Brahmi (potential interaction with sedatives).
